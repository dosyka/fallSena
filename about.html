About FallGuard

FallGuard is an IoT safety system that detects falls and alerts caretakers in under three seconds. It brings together an ESP32-C6 microcontroller, a 3-axis accelerometer, and a companion caretaker app into one simple, reliable device that watches over the people who matter most.

🎯

What it is

A compact wearable device and caretaker app that work together to keep a loved one safe at home and on the move.

⚡

What it does

It senses a fall the instant it happens and sends an alert straight to the caretaker's phone — in under three seconds.

❤

Why it matters

Fast alerts mean help arrives sooner. FallGuard gives families confidence and the people they care for their independence.

Meet the founder

Dmytro Osyka

Founder & Engineer

FallGuard started with a worry that a lot of families know well — the fear that a loved one could fall while no one is around to notice. Dmytro set out to turn that worry into something useful: a small device that simply pays attention, all the time, and speaks up the moment something goes wrong.

He designed the hardware, wrote the detection firmware, and shaped the caretaker experience end to end. FallGuard is built on a simple belief — that thoughtful technology should give families peace of mind and help the people they care for stay independent for longer.
