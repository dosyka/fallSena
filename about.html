About FallSena

FallSena is an IoT safety system that detects falls and alerts caretakers within seconds. It brings together an ESP32-C6 microcontroller, a 3-axis accelerometer, and a companion caretaker app into one simple, reliable device that watches over the people who matter most.

What it is

A compact wearable device and caretaker app that work together to keep a loved one safe at home and on the move.

What it does

It senses a fall the instant it happens and sends an alert straight to the caretaker's phone within seconds.

Why it matters

Fast alerts mean help arrives sooner. FallSena gives families confidence and the people they care for their independence.

Meet the founder

Dmytro Osyka

Founder & Engineer

I started FallSena due to a worry that many people share: that their loved one may fall down and they won’t get help in time. This is an issue that has troubled me for years. As a black belt in karate, I have come to realize how common falls are, and how dangerous they can be if someone can’t provide aid. The FallSena sensor is designed to expedite the process, bringing the user help as soon as possible by notifying the caretaker through their phone.

The hardware was designed to be affordable and reliable. The software was made to ensure a seamless and swift experience for users and caretakers alike. FallSena was built on a simple belief. Thoughtful and efficient technology should give families peace of mind and help keep those they care for safe.
