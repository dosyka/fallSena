Features

Everything FallGuard offers, built around fast and reliable fall alerts.

⚡

Real-time fall detection

Continuous accelerometer monitoring detects sudden impacts the instant they happen, day or night.

🔔

Instant push notifications

A confirmed fall triggers a push alert to the caretaker's phone in under three seconds.

📊

Device status & battery monitoring

See connection state, last-seen time, and remaining battery at a glance in the caretaker app.

🔗

Easy Bluetooth/Wi-Fi pairing

A guided setup connects the device over Bluetooth or Wi-Fi in just a few simple steps.

🔋

Low-power operation

Optimized firmware keeps power draw low so the device runs for days between charges.

🛡

Caretaker peace of mind

A clear alert history and live status give caretakers confidence that help is always one tap away.
