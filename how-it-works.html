How FallSena works

FallSena keeps things simple. The wearable senses movement, its smart detection knows a real fall from everyday life, and it calls for help fast.

  1. The wearable senses movement

    The device has a small motion sensor. As the wearer goes about their day, it quietly keeps track of how they are moving.

  2. Smart detection spots a real fall

    Built-in detection tells a genuine fall apart from everyday activity. It looks at how hard the body moves, so it avoids false alarms.

  3. It alerts the caretaker within seconds

    Once a fall is detected, the device sends a message over Bluetooth. The caretaker's phone is alerted within seconds, so help can be on the way quickly.
