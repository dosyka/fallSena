FallSena detects falls and alerts caretakers within seconds. The wearable device senses a fall the moment it happens and sends an alert straight to a caretaker's phone, ensuring the caretaker is always aware.

Designed for independence and safety

FallSena pairs a compact wearable sensor with a simple caretaker app. Here is what makes it work.

Real-time detection

An onboard accelerometer continuously measures motion and flags sudden impacts that look like a fall.

Instant alerts

When a fall is detected, a push notification reaches the caretaker's phone within seconds.

Low-power design

Efficient firmware keeps the device running for days on a single charge, with battery status always visible.
